About Us

Welcome to the Alexa Developer SRM Student Club, where innovation meets voice technology! Our club is dedicated to empowering students to explore, learn, and build applications using Amazon's Alexa platform. We provide a collaborative environment where creative minds come together to design and develop cutting-edge voice-enabled solutions that shape the future of interaction with technology. At the Alexa Developer SRM Student Club, we believe in the power of voice as the next frontier in computing. Whether you're a beginner curious about smart assistants or a seasoned developer looking to enhance your skills, our club offers a space for everyone to grow. Through hands-on workshops, hackathons, and guest lectures, we equip our members with the knowledge and tools to bring their ideas to life.

Our Company

Our Mission To foster a community of learners and innovators passionate about voice technology and to provide resources and opportunities for students to excel in voice-first development. Our Vision To be a hub of excellence in voice application development, where SRM students can drive innovation and contribute to the growing ecosystem of voice-enabled technologies.
